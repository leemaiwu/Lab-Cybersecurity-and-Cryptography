This cipher splits the alphabet in half and creates two sets of letters. A letter in the first set corresponds to a letter in the second, and vice versa.  Given an “N” the cipher would translate it to “A” and if given an “A” it would translate to “N”

Key: 

A ↔ N
B ↔ O
C ↔ P
D ↔ Q
E ↔ R
F ↔ S
G ↔ T
H ↔ U
I ↔ V
J ↔ W
K ↔ X
L ↔ Y
M ↔ Z
